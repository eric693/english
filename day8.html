Part 02

聽力練習

Listening & Dialogue
Dialogue — 機場入境 / Airport & Immigration
Officer
"Good afternoon. Passport, please." 下午好。護照請出示。
Traveler
"Here you go." 給你。
Officer
"What is the purpose of your visit?" 您此次訪問的目的是什麼？
Traveler
"Tourism. I'm here on vacation." 旅遊。我來度假的。
Officer
"How long will you be staying?" 您計劃停留多久？
Traveler
"Ten days." 十天。
Officer
"Where will you be staying?" 您會住在哪裡？
Traveler
"At the Grand Hotel in downtown." 市中心的大飯店。
Officer
"Welcome to the country. Enjoy your stay!" 歡迎入境。祝您停留愉快！

Part 04

閱讀理解

Reading Comprehension
Reading Passage
Going through airport immigration can feel stressful, but it is actually quite straightforward if you are prepared. Make sure your passport is valid for at least six months beyond your travel dates. Have your boarding pass and any required visas ready. At immigration, be honest and answer questions clearly and concisely. Officers are trained to detect nervousness, but do not mistake politeness for suspicion — most travelers are waved through quickly. After collecting your luggage, you will pass through customs. If you are carrying more than the duty-free allowance, declare it to avoid fines.
Purpose of visit: Tourism / Business / Visiting family | Length of stay: Ten days / Two weeks / One month | Accommodation: At + hotel name
海關問答常用簡短清晰的回答。被問 'How long?' 說 'Ten days.' 即可，不必說 'I will be staying for ten days.' 簡潔最重要。
