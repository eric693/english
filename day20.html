Part 02

聽力練習

Listening & Dialogue
Dialogue — 綜合情境模擬 / Final Review & Real-life Simulation
Guide
"Welcome to your final English simulation! Ready for a full travel scenario?" 歡迎來到最終英語模擬！準備好完整旅行情境了嗎？
Learner
"Yes! I've been practicing for 20 days. Let's do this!" 是的！我練習了20天。開始吧！
Guide
"Perfect. Imagine you just landed at the airport. What's first?" 很好。想像你剛抵達機場。第一步是什麼？
Learner
"I go through immigration. Purpose of visit: tourism. Staying for ten days." 我辦理入境。訪問目的：旅遊。停留十天。
Guide
"Excellent! Now you're at the hotel. How do you check in?" 很好！現在你到了飯店。怎麼辦理入住？
Learner
"Hi! I have a reservation under Chen. Is breakfast included?" 嗨！我有預約，姓名是陳。含早餐嗎？
Guide
"Perfect! Now at a restaurant — order a meal and ask for the bill." 完美！現在在餐廳——點一道菜並要求買單。
Learner
"I'd like to order the salmon. And could we have the bill when you're ready?" 我想點鮭魚。方便時可以幫我們買單嗎？
Guide
"Outstanding! You've mastered English for real life. Congratulations!" 出色！你已經掌握了真實生活中的英語。恭喜！

Part 04

閱讀理解

Reading Comprehension
Reading Passage
You have completed twenty days of English learning. You have covered greetings, numbers, directions, transportation, dining, shopping, airports, hotels, tourism, emergencies, cafes, communication, outdoor activities, professional settings, cultural etiquette, opinions, news, and storytelling. Language learning is a lifelong journey. The key to maintaining and improving your English is consistent practice. Watch English movies and TV shows with subtitles. Listen to English podcasts during your commute. Keep a journal in English. Most importantly, do not be afraid to speak. Every conversation, even imperfect ones, is a step forward. You have come a long way. Keep going!
I'm not sure I understand. Could you + clarify? | Let me rephrase that. | What I mean is... | Does that make sense?
學語言最重要的一課：犯錯是學習的一部分。用 'Pardon me?' 和 'Could you say that more slowly?' 讓自己在不懂的時候爭取時間，而不是沉默或假裝懂了。
