Part 02

聽力練習

Listening & Dialogue
Dialogue — 戶外活動 / Outdoor Activities & Sports
Visitor
"Hi! We'd like to go hiking. Can you recommend a trail?" 嗨！我們想去健行。你可以推薦一條步道嗎？
Guide
"Sure! How experienced are you?" 當然！你們的經驗如何？
Visitor
"We're beginners. Nothing too challenging." 我們是初學者。不要太難的。
Guide
"Perfect. The Maple Trail is great for beginners. It's about five kilometers." 很好。楓葉步道非常適合初學者。大約五公里。
Visitor
"How long does it take?" 需要多久？
Guide
"About two hours at a comfortable pace. The views are stunning!" 以輕鬆的速度走大約兩小時。景色非常壯觀！
Visitor
"Is there anything we should bring?" 我們需要帶什麼嗎？
Guide
"Wear good shoes, bring water and sunscreen. You're all set!" 穿好鞋，帶水和防曬乳。準備好了！

Part 04

閱讀理解

Reading Comprehension
Reading Passage
Outdoor activities are a fantastic way to experience nature and culture during your travels. Before any outdoor adventure, always check the weather forecast and inform someone of your plans. When hiking, the golden rule is 'leave no trace': take only photographs, leave only footprints. If you are renting equipment such as bicycles, kayaks, or skis, always check the equipment for damage before you leave, and report any issues to the rental company. Many popular outdoor destinations have experienced guides who can help you explore safely. Even if your English is not perfect, guides are used to helping travelers from all backgrounds.
How long does it take? | How experienced are you? | Is there a + activity + suitable for + level? | What should I bring?
'How long does it take?' 是問時間最常用的句型。回答可以是 'About two hours' 或 'It takes roughly two hours'。在活動預訂時非常實用。
