Part 02

聽力練習

Listening & Dialogue
Dialogue — 飯店入住 / Hotel Check-in & Requests
Receptionist
"Good evening! Welcome to the Grand Pacific Hotel." 晚上好！歡迎來到大太平洋酒店。
Guest
"Thank you! I have a reservation under the name Wang." 謝謝！我有預約，姓名是王。
Receptionist
"One moment... yes, a deluxe room for three nights. May I see your passport?" 稍等…是的，豪華房三晚。可以看一下護照嗎？
Guest
"Of course. Is breakfast included?" 當然。含早餐嗎？
Receptionist
"Yes, breakfast is served from six thirty to ten AM in our restaurant." 是的，早餐在我們餐廳供應，時間是早上六點半到十點。
Guest
"Perfect. Could I also request a non-smoking room with a city view?" 太好了。我可以要求不吸菸房間並且有城市景觀嗎？
Receptionist
"Absolutely! Enjoy your stay, Mr. Wang. Here's your key card." 當然！祝您住宿愉快，王先生。這是您的房卡。

Part 04

閱讀理解

Reading Comprehension
Reading Passage
Hotels are designed to make guests feel comfortable and welcome. When checking in, always have your identification and booking confirmation ready. Most hotels offer a variety of services including room service, which allows you to order food directly to your room. If you have any problems during your stay — such as a noisy neighbor, a broken appliance, or an unclean room — do not hesitate to contact the front desk. Most hotels want to resolve issues quickly to ensure guest satisfaction. Remember to check your bill carefully before checkout, as additional charges for minibar items or phone calls may have been added.
Could I have/get + noun? | Could you send/bring/fix + noun? | The [item] isn't working. | I'd like to request...
'Could I' 和 'Could you' 是飯店情境最禮貌的請求方式，比 'Can I' 更正式，展現良好的住客禮儀。
