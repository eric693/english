Part 01

核心詞彙

Key Vocabulary
It all started when...
/ɪt ɔːl ˈstɑːrtɪd wɛn/
一切開始於…
It all started when I missed my flight in Tokyo.
Out of nowhere...
/aʊt əv ˈnoʊwɛr/
突然之間，毫無預警地
Out of nowhere, a cat jumped onto our table at the restaurant.
The next thing I knew...
/ðə nɛkst θɪŋ aɪ njuː/
然後突然間…（快速轉折）
The next thing I knew, we were dancing at a local festival.
Long story short...
/lɒŋ ˈstɔːri ʃɔːrt/
長話短說…
Long story short, we ended up staying for a whole week.
You won't believe this, but...
/juː woʊnt bɪˈliːv ðɪs bʌt/
你不會相信的，但是…
You won't believe this, but I ran into my old teacher in Paris!
And that's how...
/ænd ðæts haʊ/
就這樣…（結尾句型）
And that's how a missed bus turned into the best trip of my life.
Part 02

聽力練習

Listening & Dialogue
Dialogue — 故事敘述技巧 / Storytelling & Narratives
Emma
"You look happy! What happened?" 你看起來很開心！發生什麼事了？
Jake
"Oh, you won't believe this! So it all started when I got lost in Kyoto." 哦，你不會相信的！一切開始於我在京都迷路。
Emma
"Oh no! That sounds stressful." 哦不！聽起來很有壓力。
Jake
"I thought so too. But out of nowhere, this elderly couple started helping me." 我也這麼想。但突然間，一對老夫婦開始幫助我。
Emma
"That's so sweet! What happened next?" 真的好貼心！然後呢？
Jake
"The next thing I knew, they invited me to their home for dinner!" 然後突然間，他們邀請我去他們家吃晚飯！
Emma
"No way! How was it?" 不可能！怎麼樣？
Jake
"Long story short, it was the most memorable meal of my entire trip. And that's how getting lost became the highlight of my travel!" 長話短說，那是我整個旅途中最難忘的一頓飯。就這樣，迷路變成了我旅行的亮點！

Part 04

閱讀理解

Reading Comprehension
Reading Passage
Storytelling is one of the most powerful communication skills in any language. A good story has a clear structure: a beginning that sets the scene, a middle where tension or complications arise, and an end that resolves the situation and often includes a reflection or lesson learned. The best storytellers use vivid descriptions and emotional language to bring the listener into the experience. They vary their speaking pace — slowing down for dramatic moments and speeding up during exciting parts. Practice telling small stories about everyday events to develop your English storytelling skills.
Past Simple for main events | Past Progressive for background | Past Perfect for events before the story | Signal words: suddenly, out of nowhere, the next thing I knew
英語故事的時態：用過去簡單式（went, saw, happened）說故事主線；用過去進行式（was walking, were talking）描繪背景；用過去完成式（had already left）說明更早發生的事。
